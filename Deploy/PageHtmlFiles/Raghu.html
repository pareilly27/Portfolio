Raghu Dixit Project

Re-designing identity for ‘RAGHU DIXIT PROJECT’, an Indian music band popular for their originals composition. The main focus during the design process was given to the musician/singer Raghu Dixit. He is the face of the band and I wanted to represent that to put the focus on the face of the brand. I designed special typeface for logo to give it a block like effect. Illustrations of the face of Raghu Dixit was designed as supporting elements and to help make it look like icon for various merchandise.
