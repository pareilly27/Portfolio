Dirty Drinkers

Dirty Drinkers is a vibrant soda and beverage shop that thrives on bold flavors and an equally bold brand identity. The client envisioned a playful and memorable mascot, inspiring the transformation of a soda glass into an engaging character. We paired it with custom typography and a retro-inspired, eye-catching color palette, exuding a fun, nostalgic vibe. The branding elements, from a striking logo and whimsical illustrations to immersive packaging, were meticulously designed to enhance the brand's energetic and unique identity. Together, these elements invite customers to enjoy a refreshing and memorable experience.
