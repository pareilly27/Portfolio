Bindas Begum Rockin' Raja

BBRR is an Indian restaurant in Mumbai focused on traditional Indian cuisine with a modern twist. To reflect this blend, the design features custom illustrations inspired by centuries-old architectural elements such as ghumars (tomb structures), jharukhas (enclosed balconies), Hawa Jhali windows, and intricate inlaid tile patterns found throughout India's historic palaces. Extensive research into the styles of arches, windows, and balconies led to refined, minimalist illustrations with bright colors, creating a contemporary yet culturally rooted aesthetic. Custom fonts were crafted, drawing inspiration from jharukhas and doli windows, adding a touch of ancient history to the modern ambiance."
