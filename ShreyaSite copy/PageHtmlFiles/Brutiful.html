Brutiful

For Brutiful’s brand identity, I aimed to capture the transformative journey of healing anxiety, depression, and trauma through plant medicine and macrodosing therapies. The concept centered around blending strength and softness, reflecting Brutiful’s mission to guide individuals through powerful, life-changing experiences. The visual identity features earthy textures, organic patterns, and sophisticated typography, creating a sense of grounding and holistic transformation. Every design element—from immersive print materials to refined product packaging—was crafted to embody Brutiful’s ethos of resilience and beauty in the healing process, offering a visual narrative that resonates deeply with their commitment to integrative wellness.
