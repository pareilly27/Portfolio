I'm Shreyanshi Satodia

A Graphic Designer based in Dallas, Texas, bringing bold ideas to life through colour, typography, and thoughtful design. I believe great design moves, it grabs attention, tells a story, and leaves an impression that lingers long after the first glance.

My work blends creativity with strategy, balancing vibrant visual language with clear communication. Whether it's branding, editorial layouts, packaging, or digital experiences, I build designs that feel alive, intuitive, and full of character.

I'm endlessly curious, always exploring new ways to connect ideas and people through design. When I'm not creating, you'll find me chasing inspiration in everyday moments, and usually saying hi to every dog along the way.

Let’s create work that moves people.
