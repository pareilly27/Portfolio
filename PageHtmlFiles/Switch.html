Switch Glitch

Switch Glitch is a customisable wallpaper swatch collection kit inspired by a collection of Beer cans. This collections believes that a person’s surrounding is an extension of their personality, and thus it is important to have an environment which let’s you feel at ease and create a space that drives you. Each illustration has multiple transparent sheets that people can mix and match by layering on each other and putting it on the illustrations to create a custom colour pallet for your space.
