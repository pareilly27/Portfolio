Terrible!! Thank's for asking...

"Terrible..! Thanks for asking." is a social action campaign designed to challenge society's misconceptions about mental health. We often hear and say "Hi, how are you doing?" daily, yet it rarely prompts a genuine response. This campaign aims to reclaim the essence of this phrase by encouraging people to pause, reflect, and provide authentic answers. By sparking conversations around everyday expressions, it highlights how quickly meaningful words can lose impact without proper context and emotion. Prompt cards placed across campus collected responses from students, creating a collection that reflects their true feelings and experiences.
